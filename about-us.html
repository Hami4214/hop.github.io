Who We Are About House Of Professionals

House of Professionals (HOP) is one of the Best Training Institute in Lahore, Pakistan.

Background

House of Professionals (HOP) was established in 2009, with the aim of achieving economic growth by promoting the best specialist knowledge through commerce, engineering and information technology. HOP believes that there is a need to connect technical and economic institutions in order to foster an atmosphere of cooperation and industrial development.

Guided by the mission of assisting organizations and individuals to actualize their potential to its best on the way to meet the challenges of ever evolving world. House of Professionals (HOP) offers various training and services in a number of fields.

Vision and Mission

It describes the existence of HOP. For both Nations and Individuals, Economic Prosperity is the key element.For Individual it is to have quality life by also keeping the elements of Responsibility, Empowerment, Equilibrium, Endowment and Almsgiving.For Nations it is to be competitive in the world, grow to strengthen industries, create jobs, and encourage economic investment and entrepreneurship.

House of Professionals (HOP) work to polish and to lead the experts and organizations by providing best professional knowledge and practices.

Why students love House Of professionals ?

HOP is building a community that develops empower developers and connect them to solutions that enable productivity, growth, and discovery because we believe on maxim ”Skills for Success ”.
